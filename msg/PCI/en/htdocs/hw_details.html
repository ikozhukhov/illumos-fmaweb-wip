The Message ID:  %MSGID% indicates a problem with a device on the PCI bus. The faulty component(s), as shown with fmdump, should be repaired in order to restore the system.

Arrangements should be made to replace the Field Replaceable Unit (FRU) on which the suspect device is located. Refer to the information displayed in the FRU: field of the fmdump output. For example:

    FRU: hc://product-id=SUNW,Sun-Fire-280R/motherboard=0

If there are multiple FRU's indicted, the FRU with the highest probability should be replaced first.
