If a service is enabled and its dependencies are met, the %DISTRO% Service Manager will send a start command to the service's restarter. If the service's restarter is the %DISTRO% Service Manager (svc:/system/svc/restarter:default), it will execute the service's start method. For most services, when the method completes successfully, the service will be placed in the online state.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-C4 indicates that the transition from offline to online is in progress. This outcome can indicate a booting or restarting system, or a defect in %DISTRO% or third party software.

If the service does not leave the offline state, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information.
