The restarter for a service may determine that the service is operational, but not at full capacity. When it does so, it places the service in the degraded state.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-DX indicates that the administrator has requested that the service be marked as degraded.

If running svcadm clear service_fmri fails to clear the degraded state, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information.
