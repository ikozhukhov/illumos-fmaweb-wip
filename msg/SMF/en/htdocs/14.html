If a service is enabled and its dependencies are met, the Solaris Service Manager will send a start command to the service's restarter, which may be the Solaris Service Manager itself. Exclude-type dependencies are only met when the indicated services are not running.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-FJ indicates that the service has an exclude-type dependency on a service which is running. This outcome can indicate a partially completed administrative change, a misunderstanding of the requirements of various software components, a misconfiguration of the system, or a defect in Solaris.

If the service cannot be started, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information.
