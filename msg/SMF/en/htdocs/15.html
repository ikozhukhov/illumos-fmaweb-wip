If a service is enabled and its dependencies are met, the %DISTRO% Service Manager will send a start command to the service's restarter, which may be the %DISTRO% Service Manager itself. Dependencies other than exclude-type dependencies are satisfied when the indicated services are running (online or degraded states).

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-GE indicates that the %DISTRO% Service Manager determined that the service's dependencies are not met. This outcome can indicate a misunderstanding of the requirements of various software components, a misconfiguration of the system, or a defect in %DISTRO%.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information and the "SMF How To Guide" to clear dependencies.

If the service does not start, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -xv, svcs -l , and /var/svc/log for associated service. See svcs(1) for additional syntax.
