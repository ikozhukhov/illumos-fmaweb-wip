The Solaris Service Manager uses the dependencies of a service to determine when the service should be started. Dependencies which form a cycle are considered invalid. The Message ID:   SMF-8000-HP indicates that the Solaris Service Manager placed the service into maintenance for this reason. This outcome can indicate a misunderstanding of the requirements of various software components, a misconfiguration of the system, or a defect in Solaris.

If the service cannot be restored, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x, the relevant log messages from /var/adm/messages, and, for each service in the dependency cycle, output from svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information.
