The Solaris Service Manager uses the dependencies of a service to decide when to start it. Dependencies are read from the from the running snapshot of a service's configuration.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-JA indicates that the Solaris Service Manager has determined that the service's dependency configuration is invalid and placed the service into the maintenance state. This outcome can indicate a misunderstanding of the requirements of various software components, a misconfiguration of the system, or a defect in Solaris or third party software.

If the service cannot be corrected, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information.
