The Solaris Service Manager issues commands for a service to its responsible restarter, which may be the Solaris Service Manager itself. If a service's general/restarter property identifies a service which is not present on the system, then the Service Manager will leave the service in the uninitialized state.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-3P indicates that the service cannot be started for the above reason. This outcome can indicate a misunderstanding of the requirements of various software components, a misconfiguration of the system, or a defect in Solaris.

The recommended action for the system administrator is to attempt the remedy given in the Suggested Action. In the case of failure, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information.
