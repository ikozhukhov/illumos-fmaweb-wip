The %DISTRO% Service Manager issues commands for a service to its responsible restarter, which may be the %DISTRO% Service Manager itself. When a new service is created, the Service Manager initializes its state to uninitialized and instructs the restarter to read the service's configuration and update its state.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-4D indicates that the service's restarter has not read the service's configuration and updated its state.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information and reference "SMF How To Guide".
