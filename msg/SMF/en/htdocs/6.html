If an error occurs while manipulating a service, the %DISTRO% Service Manager will place the service in the maintenance state and will not start the service.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-63 indicates that an administrator has requested that the %DISTRO% Service Manager place the service in the maintenance state. Typically this is done with svcadm mark maintenance service_fmri.

If the Suggested Action does not work, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information and reference the "SMF How To Guide".
