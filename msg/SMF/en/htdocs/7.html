The %DISTRO% Service Manager attempts to activate services on a %DISTRO% system based on a graph of dependencies and administrator intentions, using a defined set of service methods. In the case that a service method fails multiple times in succession, the Service Manager will place the service in the maintenance state until administrator repairs can be carried out.

The Message ID:   SMF-8000-7Y indicates that the %DISTRO% Service Manager has classified a service in this way.

This outcome can indicate a misunderstanding of the requirements of various software components, a misconfiguration of the system, or a defect in %DISTRO%. The recommended action for the system administrator is to attempt the remedy given in the Suggested Action above. In the case of failure, contact Sun Service with the output of svcs -x and svcs -l impacted_service_fmri.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information and reference the "SMF How To Guide" the section entitled "Retrieving Dependency Tree Information". This provides detailed information for troubleshooting service dependency issues.

If the above Action doesn't help, use the instructions in document 203634 to place svc.startd in debug mode and contact your service provider.
