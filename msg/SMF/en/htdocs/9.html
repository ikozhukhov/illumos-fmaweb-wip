When a restarter places a service in the maintenance state, it may supply an explanation. The Message ID:   SMF-8000-9C indicates that the restarter for the service did not supply an explanation. If svc:/system/svc/restarter:default is the restarter, this condition generally indicates a defect in %DISTRO%.

See "Predictive Self-Healing" for additional information, also placing "svc.startd in debug mode" will provide more information in the /var/svc/log/svc.startd.log file.
