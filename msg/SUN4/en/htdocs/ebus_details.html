The Message ID:  %MSGID% indicates a device connected to the Ebus is faulty and requires Service. The current Ebus transaction is terminated and the Ebus continues to operate.

Use the fmdump -v -u EVENT_ID command to determine the faulty component as shown in the example below. 
# fmdump -v -u  1dd03a08-3f16-c3d9-8b11-ada871c07a3b
TIME                 UUID                                 SUNW-MSG-ID
Nov 17 19:04:03.0975 1dd03a08-3f16-c3d9-8b11-ada871c07a3b SUN4-8000-13
   50%  fault.io.ebus
         FRU: hc://product-id=SUNW,Sun-Fire-T200/component=IOBD
        rsrc: hc:///ioboard=0/hostbridge=0/pciexrc=0

   50%  fault.io.ebus
         FRU: hc://product-id=SUNW,Sun-Fire-T200/component=IOBD
        rsrc: hc:///ioboard=0/hostbridge=0/pciexrc=1


Use the fmdump -eV -u EVENT_ID command to collect the ereports associated with this problem.

Have this information available when contacting Sun Services for a repair.
