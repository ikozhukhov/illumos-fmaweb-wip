The Message ID:   %MSGID% indicates the %DISTRO% Fault Manager has received reports from a CPU that one or more errors associated with the dcache, icache or L2 data cache have been detected. Automatic diagnosis has determined that the errors reported indicate a fault requiring repair action is present.

Arrangements should be made to replace the Field Replaceable Unit (FRU) on which the suspect CPU is located. Refer to the information displayed in the FRU: field of the fmdump output. For example:

    FRU: legacy-hc:///component=Slot A
