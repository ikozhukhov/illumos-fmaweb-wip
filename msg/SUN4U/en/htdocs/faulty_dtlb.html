The Message ID:   %MSGID% indicates that the %DISTRO% Fault Manager has received reports from a CPU that one or more errors associated with the dtlb have been detected. The Diagnostic Engine (DE) has triggered an automatic FMA response to disable and isolate this CPU from the configuration in order to prevent repeat errors and increase the system's total Availability.

Arrangements should be made to replace the Field Replaceable Unit (FRU) on which the suspect CPU is located. Refer to the information displayed in the FRU: field of the fmdump output. For example:

    FRU: legacy-hc:///component=Slot A
