The Message ID:  %MSGID% indicates a device connected to the hostbridge bus is faulty and requires Service. The system may attempt to disable the faulty device and allow the rest of the system to continue operation. Arrangements should be made to replace the Field Replaceable Unit (FRU) on which the suspect resource is located.

Refer to the information displayed in the FRU: field of the fmdump -v -u EVENT_ID command to determine the faulty components as shown in the example below. 

# fmdump -v -u 719ff23c-9828-6c65-9099-ad3bf0a07c40
TIME                 UUID                                 SUNW-MSG-ID
Dec 31 21:40:07.0023 719ff23c-9828-6c65-9099-ad3bf0a07c40 SUN4U-8001-DA
  100%  fault.io.hbus
         FRU: hc:///ioboard=0/hostbridge=0
        rsrc: hc:///ioboard=0/hostbridge=0
Have this information available when contacting Sun Services for a repair.

Note: You may also be requested to provide a copy of the files in the /var/fm/fmd directory and also a copy of the output from the fmadm config command.
